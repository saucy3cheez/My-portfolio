About Dr. Shetty

A Surgeon Shaped
by Dedication

Dr. Saurav Shetty K is a General Surgeon (MS) with comprehensive training from Dr. D. Y. Patil Medical College, Hospital & Research Centre, Pune — one of India's foremost tertiary care centres. Currently serving as a Senior Resident, Dr. Shetty brings operative independence across a wide spectrum of surgical procedures combined with a thoughtful, patient-centric approach to care.

With rotational exposure across Urology, GI Surgery, CTVS, Surgical ICU, and Surgical Oncology, and a published thesis on Anterior Abdominal Wall Hernias, Dr. Shetty embodies the rigour of academic surgery paired with the sensitivity that cosmetic consultancy demands — with a particular appreciation for the diversity and beauty of Indian skin.
